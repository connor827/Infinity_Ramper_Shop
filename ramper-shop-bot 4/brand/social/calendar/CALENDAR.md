## POSTING NOTES

- Keep tweets under 280 characters when possible. The copy above is already within limits.
- Post with the image in the same tweet, not as a reply.
- Don't tag competitors directly — strong opinions are good, personal attacks aren't.
- Engage with any reply to your own posts within 6 hours. Algorithm heavily favours posts that get quick author responses.
- Quote-tweet relevant news with a one-line take — adds to your "always on" signal without requiring original content.
- Don't worry about follower count in the first 12 weeks. Worry about making each post something a payments-interested person would screenshot.
